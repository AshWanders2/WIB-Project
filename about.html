About AEIS

Aegis Endpoint Integrity Systems introduces a new model for consumer cybersecurity: sovereignty, visibility, and lawful preservation of digital identity.

Addressing Critical Gaps

The consumer cybersecurity market has historically centered on reactive antivirus tools tied to vendor-operated cloud systems. AEIS addresses these deficiencies directly by placing evidence control and integrity at the center of its design.

Delivering Practical Outcomes

  • Improved visibility — meaningful endpoint activity, less noise.
  • Tamper-evident records — preserved for independent verification.
  • Controlled export — standardized formats for lawful use.

Philosophy & Approach

AEIS emphasizes transparency and sovereignty. Users retain ownership of their evidentiary posture, ensuring records are not filtered, stored, or altered by third parties. The system observes and records activity in a non-intrusive, neutral chain-of-custody posture.

Broader Context

AEIS aligns with growing demands for stronger privacy rights and regulatory standards requiring verifiable evidence. The architecture equips individuals with tools proportionate to modern adversaries — once reserved for state or enterprise operations.

About the Team

AEIS is developed and maintained under the ARCHE framework. The mission: bring enterprise-grade defensive outcomes to everyday users through anticipatory intelligence, forensic rigor, and user sovereignty.
